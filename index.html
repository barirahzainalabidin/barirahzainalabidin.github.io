About Me

Hi, I'm Barirah Zainal Abidin from Malaysia. I'm currently a Machine Learning Practitioner on Kaggle, but my journey here has been far from simple. It all started in 2016 when I ventured into entrepreneurship, running five different businesses. I sold perfumes, scarves, and wholesale products from Alibaba and local suppliers. For me, business wasn’t just about making money, it was something I truly enjoyed. I loved exploring market trends, trying out new ideas, and finding what strategies worked best. Over the past 7 years, I’ve gained valuable experience in advertising and collaborative marketing. I’ve worked with influencers and artists across Malaysia, which helped me grow my skills in promoting products effectively.
